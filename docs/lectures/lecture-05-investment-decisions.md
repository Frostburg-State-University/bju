# Lecture 5 — Investment Decisions & Alternative Selection

!!! info "Session Info"
    **Date:** Sunday, 2026-06-07 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Time:** 16:20–18:10 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Room:** SY109
    **Instructor:** Dr. Zhijiang Chen

## Learning Objectives

1. Compute and interpret **NPV**, **IRR**, **payback period**, and **Profitability Index (PI)**.
2. Choose between **mutually exclusive** vs. **independent** alternatives.
3. Apply **incremental IRR analysis** and the **Minimum Attractive Rate of Return (MARR)**.
4. Decide between Build-In-House vs. SaaS for the motivating example from Lecture 1.

## Key Concepts

- **NPV decision rule:** accept if $NPV > 0$.
- **IRR decision rule:** accept if $IRR > MARR$.
- **Conflicts** between NPV and IRR: when they disagree, NPV wins.
- **Incremental analysis** for mutually exclusive alternatives.
- **Capital budgeting** under a budget constraint — rank by PI.

## In-Class Exercise

Revisit the Elasticsearch vs. SaaS-search example from Lecture 1. At a 10% MARR, compute NPV for both alternatives over five years and recommend one. Then redo at 6% and 14% — does your decision flip?
