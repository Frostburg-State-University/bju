## Learning Objectives

1. Draw and interpret a **cash flow diagram**.
2. Apply uniform-series, arithmetic-gradient, and geometric-gradient formulas.
3. Establish **equivalence** between cash-flow streams under a given interest rate.
4. Decompose realistic software-project cash flows into standard patterns.

## Key Formulas

- **Uniform series PV ($P/A$):** $PV = A \cdot \dfrac{(1 + i)^n - 1}{i \, (1 + i)^n}$
- **Uniform series FV ($F/A$):** $FV = A \cdot \dfrac{(1 + i)^n - 1}{i}$
- **Arithmetic gradient PV:** standard $P/G$ factor.
- **Geometric gradient PV:** closed-form expression for cash flows growing at rate $g$.
