# Lecture 14 — Student Project Presentations (Part 1)

!!! info "Session Info"
    **Date:** Tuesday, 2026-06-16 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Time:** 16:20–18:10 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Room:** YF108
    **Instructor:** Dr. Zhijiang Chen

## Format

- **Time per group:** 12 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A + 3 minutes peer evaluation.
- Roughly half the groups present today; the remainder present in Lecture 15.

## Required Content of Each Presentation

1. Project description and scope.
2. Cost estimate (Function Points or COCOMO II), with assumptions.
3. Cash-flow forecast and at least two alternatives.
4. NPV, IRR, payback, PI for each alternative.
5. Sensitivity or risk analysis (Tornado, decision tree, or Monte Carlo).
6. **(Bonus)** AI-cost dimension.
7. Final recommendation with stakeholder justification.

## Peer Evaluation Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|-------:|
| Clarity and structure | 20% |
| Methodological correctness | 30% |
| Use of sensitivity / risk analysis | 20% |
| Persuasiveness of recommendation | 20% |
| Q&A handling | 10% |

## Instructor Grading

The instructor's rubric mirrors the peer rubric with two additions: (a) data realism, (b) integration of AI economics where relevant.
