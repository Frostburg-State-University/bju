# Lecture 9 — Cost Estimation II: COCOMO II

!!! info "Session Info"
    **Date:** Thursday, 2026-06-11 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Time:** 16:20–18:10 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Room:** YF302
    **Instructor:** Dr. Zhijiang Chen

## Learning Objectives

1. Explain the lineage from COCOMO 81 to **COCOMO II**.
2. Distinguish the **Early Design** and **Post-Architecture** models.
3. Compute effort using the COCOMO II equation:
   $\text{PM} = A \cdot \text{Size}^{E} \cdot \prod_{i=1}^{n} \text{EM}_i$
   where $E = B + 0.01 \sum SF_j$.
4. Interpret **scale factors** and **effort multipliers**.

## Topics

- COCOMO II equation walkthrough.
- The five Scale Factors: PREC, FLEX, RESL, TEAM, PMAT.
- The 17 Effort Multipliers (Post-Architecture).
- Calibration and local adjustment.
- Translating Function Points to KSLOC.

## In-Class Exercise

Convert the Function Point estimate from Lecture 8 to KSLOC and compute effort in person-months under three scenarios: nominal, optimistic, pessimistic.
